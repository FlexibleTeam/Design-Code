About This Article

First of all, its not so much an article as it is a collection of short musical loops created on my phone while on the train and walking to work using the PropellorHead Figure app.

I like music, I like to listen to it. I like to have it on and not listen to it, and I even like to create it every now and then. What was so much fun about this app is that the creation is so easy and most everything you make sounds at least passible. The app keps you on time and in key, the rest is up to you. The UI is fantastic and very out-of-the-box for an ios app. I also like the ridiculous names it suggests for each new track.

Please enjoy these Train Ride Jams. Let me know which one is your favorite in the comments!
