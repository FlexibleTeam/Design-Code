Making Friends

Why is it so difficult to make new friends?

I find the best way to meet people is to not fear them. Look them in the eye, tell them what you have to say and be genuinely interested in how they respond.

The next time you have the opportunity to meet someone new, or shrink away in defeat; be brave, and do not fear rejection. You will find a surprising strength within you, and you will meet someone new.
