Planting New Roots

It appears this next leg of our journey has planted us at the foot of some of the tallest living organisms on the planet. It feels just like yesterday that we packed everything we owned on earth into a twelve foot trailer and drove west.

After seven-ish months working with the very talented people at Sparkart, a new opportunity that I could not pass up presented itself to me and I am excited to have joined up with Mashlogic. We have an excellent crew and are launching some of our first products soon.

— June 17th, 2011
