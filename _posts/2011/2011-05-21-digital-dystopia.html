This song's content/theme was inspired by Jamiroquai's 'Virtual Insanity', a song about how we as a people are getting more lost as our technology advances. Musically by Kanye West when he pushes what is normally expected from pop and rap music, blending them and distorting them to create something new. And Finally the rapid vocal stylings are a nod to the east coast rap I grew up with. MC's like the Rza and Busta Rhymes, how they would double and triple the tempo of the music with the lyrics and find new rhythms inside the beat.

On it's highest level it's about a struggle to maintain personal relationships in this digital world. To pit interactivity versus inter-personality. Digital identities versus organic realities.

On a lower level, it's about one character who amasses almost god-like powers through technological means. And the response of other people who are caught in the wake of that power.

At it's most basic level it's an awesome jam and some pretty wild lyrics that I hope people can get into.
